about

Masha is a product designer currently living in Providence, RI.

She works at LogicManager, an enterprise risk management SaaS company, which she joined in 2018 as a software engineer and then became their first designer.

Before that, she was studying computer science at Brandeis University.

Masha loves design systems, creative coding, and data visualization. Occasionally she writes on Medium and publishes resources on Figma.

Outside of product design, she enjoys reading about urban planning, psychology, and health. Check out her virtual bookshelf on Oku.

She's really into yoga, strength training, running, and taking long walks.

Masha speaks Russian and English fluently and knows some Spanish, Italian, and German.

Reach out to her at mash@hey.com or say hi on IG.
