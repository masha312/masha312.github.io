Summary

Based on the above examples, let‚Äôs compile a list of suggestions for nutrition calculator best practices:

  1. Consider integrating complete nutrition calculator (calories, macros) into the ordering process.
  2. At the same time, also consider having a standalone nutrition calculator feature.
  3. Include calories for each menu item/ingredient.
  4. Include macros for each menu item/ingredient.
  5. Display calories for the entire order (or at the very least, for each separate menu item).
  6. Update calories and macros as the user customizes their order.
  7. Keep calories and macros information visible as the user customizes their order.
  8. Make sure nutrition calculator (whether it‚Äôs a separate feature or if it‚Äôs integrated with ordering) is accessible via the app and on the website (both mobile and desktop versions).

Of course, as seen in cases above, not every single one of these has to be implemented in order to have a great app! These suggestions are solely based on my opinion and what I personally find helpful to stay on track with my goals.
